### Client Work: Capstone Presentations

Present your capstone and demonstrate the full OS in action.

```bash
Help me prepare for my capstone presentation:

**Pre-Presentation Checklist**

**Storyline**
- [ ] Our main recommendation is crystal clear
- [ ] The SCQA flow makes sense
- [ ] Every slide has an action title
- [ ] The "so what" is obvious

**Team Coordination**
- [ ] Everyone knows their section
- [ ] Handoffs are smooth and practiced
- [ ] We have consistent messaging
- [ ] We've rehearsed with timing

**Q&A Preparation**
- [ ] We've listed the 10 hardest questions we might get
- [ ] We know who answers what
- [ ] We have backup data ready
- [ ] We've practiced handling pushback

**Delivery**
- [ ] Each person knows their key points cold
- [ ] We're making eye contact, not reading slides
- [ ] Our pace is appropriate (not rushing)
- [ ] We sound confident but not arrogant

**Last-Minute Polish**
What final improvements should we make to the deck or our delivery?

**Post-Presentation Reflection**
After presenting:
- What went well?
- What would we do differently?
- What feedback did we receive?
- How did we demonstrate *The Consultant's OS*?
```
